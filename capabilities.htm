Competitive Advantage

Here at JAH Machine, we can manufacture your CNC machined parts from simple to complex. Whether it’s one part or one hundred, our advanced CNC machining process is designed for efficiency and speed. With the application of our various CNC capabilities, your custom CNC part will be tooled onsite at our facility. So when quality, timing and pricing are critical, we can deliver the parts you need to keep your projects moving forward.

Unlike many of our competitors, your CNC machined part will be made right here in the U.S. from start to finish. We combine the latest CNC machining and turning processes with proprietary technology to deliver high quality, on-demand parts.

What makes us different from our competition?

JAH Machine is known for its expedited machining services while maintaining our high quality assurance standards. We are AS9100 Rev D certified precision machine shop and have an excellent reputation with our customers. With that said, we are known for producing high quality machined parts with extremely tight tolerances, successful delivery of complex projects, short lead times and superior customer service. These are some of the factors that contribute to our high customer satisfaction results.

You might be asking your self how can they have such fast turn around times? We are able to quickly produce our customers parts because we make our own custom tooling & fixtures that are necessary to meet our customers’ project requirements. This allows us to even accommodate same day or next day delivery (including weekends) depending on the requirements.
