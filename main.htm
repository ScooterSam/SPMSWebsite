Seattle Precision Machine Shop, Inc.  is a solutions-oriented contract machine shop that operates on a lean, just-in-time basis to deliver the ultimate in quality, pricing, and delivery. We possess an arsenal of high tech equipment, including five new Mazak Integrex 5-axis multitasking mill-turn machines with features such as automatic bar feeders, pallet changers, and in-process gaging. We also operate a considerable lineup of horizontal and vertical machining centers as well as 4 Flexible Manufacturing systems, the largest featuring 6 HMC’s, 120 Pallets, and 2088 tools. We can machine complex parts from 2 ounces to 2,000 pounds. We will ensure our customers have clean, burr free parts every time.

 

With our state-of-the-art equipment, we leverage the power and versatility of our tools to produce parts that comply with challenging design and quality criteria. Focusing on the principles of automation and precision, and combining that with authoritative knowledge of high-performance materials, we have exceptional competencies in the area of CNC machining aircraft and aerospace components. Our large multi-axis machines have allowed us to excel at Oil and Gas components. With solid process methodology and innovation, we are also a leading supplier of precision hydraulic components. We have extensive knowledge of manifold and fluid power component materials and utilize machining methods that dramatically decrease production time while achieving zero-defect results.

 

We manufacture parts to exacting requirements by adhering to the principles of Six Sigma and our ISO 9001:2015 quality management systems. Our engineering, quality, and production staff work as a team to ensure we meet or exceed expectations. Along with SPC analysis of our processes, we inspect parts using advanced metrology systems including 7 Zeiss CMM’s and thousands of top quality hand gages. With nearly 200 dedicated, well trained team members, we have the size and talent to execute large programs seamlessly.
